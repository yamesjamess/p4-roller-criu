About Roller Criú

We are the only roller skating club in Ireland. Let's rock and roll!

How we got started

Roller Criú, a pioneering roller skating initiative, was born out of Roisin Smith's passion and determination in the year 2020. Faced with the frustrating realization that there was no formal establishment dedicated to teaching the art of roller skating, Roisin decided to take matters into her own hands. Fuelled by her love for roller skating and a desire to share it with others, she founded Roller Criú. This unique venture was designed to cater to everyone, from those taking their first tentative steps on wheels to those aiming for competition-level skills. Through a combination of dedication, innovative teaching methods, and a welcoming atmosphere, Roller Criú has since become a thriving community and a go-to destination for roller skaters of all levels, ensuring that the joy of roller skating is accessible to all.

Roisin Smith, Founder & Head Coach
